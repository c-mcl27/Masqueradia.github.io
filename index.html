About Us

History

The annual festival in the University of limerick...

The annual festival in the University of limerick has been a key part of our students experience these last fifty years. This all began as a modest campus get together in the mid 70s and quickly become one of the most exciting events of the year for the whole country. Every spring, students from all over the country come to stay and participate in the event of the year making our campus the center of music and community spirit. From early appearances by U2 and The Cranberries to performances by Dermot Kennedy and Hozier. It’s always an exciting time every year for the wait of our artist lineups and we somehow manage to never disappoint. Don’t miss the moment, run with the wolves!
