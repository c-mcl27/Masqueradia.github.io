Plan Your Journey Around Masqueradia


Masqueradia Festival takes place annually on the University of Limerick's campus in County Limerick, Ireland.



Due to the location of this festival, it has become a very popular annual event for the students that attend the University. It provides students with something to anticipate and look forward to post-exam season! Despite its popularity between University students, the festival is inclusive and anyone over 18 years old is encouraged to attend!


Directions Around the Festival

UL campus can be confusing to navigate, but with the below guide (and the maps dotted throughout the festival), we will try to reduce the amount of times you get turned around over the weekend.


Toilets

There are potable toilets located beside the White House building and in the courtyard directly before the Living Bridge. There are also indoor toilets available in Stables and inside the Concert Hall.

Food Stalls and Beverages

Food stalls are arranged around UL, offering a variety of foods such as pizza, chicken, fries, burgers and other finger foods to keep you going over the weekend. There are also vegan, vegetarian and gluten-free options available. There are benches beside each food stall where you can enjoy your food! Stables offers food between 6-9pm, but there are limited seats inside the restraunt.

Alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages can also be purchased at these stalls, and will be served in either plasic cups or plastic bottles. There are water fountains located outside the Foundation Building and Glucksman library to keep you hydrated throughout the day!

Stages

White House Stage: Located on the lawn directly beside the UL White House building.

Concert Hall Stage: Situated inside the UL Concert Hall.
